Summary[edit | edit source]

Products are useful or valuable material. Some of the products can be crafted in the player's inventory using blueprints and the proper ingredients. Most of the blueprints can be found in Operations Centres or Manufacturing Facilities unless noted otherwise.

See tradeable for products that cannot be crafted.
