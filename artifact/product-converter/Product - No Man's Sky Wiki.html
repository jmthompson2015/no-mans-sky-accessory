Summary[edit | edit source]

A product is a useful or valuable material. Products can be crafted in a player's inventory using blueprints and the proper resources. Most of the recipes can be found in Operations Centres or Manufacturing Facilities unless noted otherwise.

See trade commodity for items that cannot be crafted.

Types of products[edit | edit source]

  • Antimatter (blueprint from Awakenings)
  • Antimatter Housing (blueprint from Awakenings)
  • Carbon Nanotubes (always craftable)
  • Cobalt Mirror
  • Hermetic Seal (blueprint from Awakenings)
  • Metal Plating (always craftable)
